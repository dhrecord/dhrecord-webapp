About

DHRecord is an online Dental Health Record Management System that is designed to connect dental clinics and patients. Our main purpose is to provide a unique and outstanding service as compared to the other web apps in the market. Especially for dental clinics business owners that wants to have their business being hosted on a website so that customers can find their clinic and book an appointment easily.

With this system, it will increase the productivity of our users as all relevant and useful information of our clients' customers will be collated into our database to make it easier for the users to search for patients' medical history, drug allergies, treatments undergone, etc. Other than that, our system will also show a list of our clients' clinics with their specialization and the distance from their location to the clinic.

Main Features that we offer:
Record Management
Clinic Search
Appointment Booking
Inventory Management
Referral Tracking
Treatment History Search
Bill Generator
Benefit for Registered Dental Clinics:

As a registered dental clinics, you will be provided with features to help organize all patients' records and make it efficient for users of our software to search for required information of their patient.


Benefit for Registered Users (Patients):

As a registered user (patient), you will be provided with an easy, secure, and fast access to clinic search and appointment booking.
